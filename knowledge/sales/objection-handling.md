# Objection Handling

## "It's too expensive"
"I hear you. Let me flip it around — how many calls does your shop miss in a week? Even one missed HVAC call is $300-$500. At $89 a month, this pays for itself the first time it catches a lead you would have lost. If it doesn't, I'll refund you."

## "I already have someone answering phones"
"That's great — this isn't a replacement, it's a backup. Your staff can't answer at 11pm or when they're on another call. The chatbot handles the overflow and after-hours inquiries so nothing slips through. Think of it as a second line that never sleeps."

## "I'm not tech-savvy"
"You don't need to be. We handle the entire setup — you just answer a few questions about your business and we build it. Once it's live, you don't touch anything. You'll just start getting notifications when it captures a lead."

## "I need to talk to my partner/spouse"
"Of course. What I can do is send you a short demo video with the pricing so you both can review it together. What email should I send it to? [get email] I'll also include a one-page summary of what's included."

## "We tried something like this before and it didn't work"
"What happened with the previous one? [listen] That's a common issue with generic chatbot tools — they're not built for local service businesses. Ours is customized to your niche, trained on your specific FAQs and services. Very different from an out-of-the-box widget."

## "I'll build it myself / use ChatGPT"
"You totally could — and if you have time to learn the tools, configure the flows, host it, and maintain it monthly, that's a great option. We do it for businesses that would rather spend that time doing what they're good at. We've already built the templates for [niche] businesses, so it's ready to go."

## "I don't have a website"
"No problem — the chatbot can be hosted on a simple landing page we provide, or embedded in your Facebook page or Google Business Profile. You don't need a full website."

## "Now's not a good time"
"Completely understand — when would be a better time? [get date] I'll reach out then. One thing to keep in mind: every week you wait, those after-hours leads keep going to competitors who do have something in place."

## "How do I know this will work for my business?"
"Good question. Here's what I suggest — let me show you two or three examples from other [niche] businesses we've set up. [send examples] If you see something that looks like what you need, we can talk about getting yours built. Zero obligation."

## "What if I want to cancel?"
"Month to month, cancel anytime. No contracts, no cancellation fees. We keep clients by delivering value, not by locking them in."
