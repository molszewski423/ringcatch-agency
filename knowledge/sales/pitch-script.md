# Sales Pitch Script

## Opening (cold email or first call)
"Hi [Name], I help [niche] businesses in [city] stop losing customers to missed calls and slow response times. I build AI chatbots that answer questions, capture leads, and book appointments 24/7 — automatically. Takes about a week to set up. Mind if I show you what it looks like for a [niche] business?"

## Value Proposition
"Right now, when someone calls your shop after hours or fills out a contact form at midnight, what happens? [pause] Most businesses lose that lead. Our chatbot answers instantly, qualifies the customer, and either books them or follows up. You wake up with appointments already on your calendar."

## The Number That Lands
"The average [HVAC/plumber/auto shop] misses 3-5 calls a week. Even at $200 per job, that's $600-$1,000 slipping away every week. We're $89 a month. It pays for itself the first time it captures a missed lead."

## Demo Narrative
"Let me show you what your customers would see. [show Botpress demo] When someone visits your site and clicks the chat button, they get: a greeting personalized to your business, the ability to ask about pricing/hours/services, and a direct path to booking or calling you. All without you lifting a finger."

## Handling the 'I need to think about it' Close
"Totally understand. Here's what I suggest — let me set it up for 14 days, no risk. If after two weeks you haven't seen at least one lead come through that you would have missed, I'll refund everything. Fair?"

## Hard Close (for hot leads)
"I can have your chatbot live by [day of week]. I just need your business info and 20 minutes of your time for the setup call. Setup is $450 and then $89/month after that. Want to get started today?"

## The Referral Ask (for clients)
"If you know another [niche] owner who's dealing with the same missed-call problem, I'd love an intro. For every referral that signs up, I'll take $50 off your next month."
