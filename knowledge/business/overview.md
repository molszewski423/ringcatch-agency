# RingCatch Business Overview

## What We Do
RingCatch sells AI chatbots to local small businesses. The chatbot answers customer questions 24/7, captures leads, books appointments, and handles FAQs — replacing the missed calls and after-hours losses that small businesses bleed every day.

## Business Model
- **Setup fee:** $450 one-time (covers build, configuration, and first-month support)
- **Monthly recurring:** $89/month (hosting, maintenance, updates, support)
- **Target:** 100 active clients = $8,900 MRR / $106,800 ARR

## Why It Works
Local service businesses (HVAC, plumbers, restaurants, auto repair) miss 30-40% of inbound calls. Each missed call is a lost job worth $200-$2,000. A $89/month chatbot that captures even 2 extra leads per month pays for itself 10x over.

## Current Status
- Pre-revenue, actively building pipeline
- Automated lead generation via Google Maps scraper (75 leads/day max)
- Outreach via Brevo email (cold → warm → booked sequence)
- Delivery pipeline: Botpress chatbot JSON + PDF onboarding guide

## Key Metrics to Track
- MRR (goal: $500 by month 1, $2,000 by month 3, $8,900 by month 12)
- Lead → Client conversion rate (target: 2%)
- Email open rate (benchmark: 21%)
- Churn rate (target: <5%/month)
- Avg client LTV: 24 months × $89 = $2,136 + $450 setup = $2,586

## Competitive Advantage
- Fully automated delivery — no manual build time per client
- Local focus — not competing with enterprise chatbot vendors
- Low price point removes objection friction
- AI-personalized outreach at scale
