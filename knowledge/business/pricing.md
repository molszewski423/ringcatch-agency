# RingCatch Pricing Strategy

## Standard Pricing
- **Setup:** $450 (one-time)
- **Monthly:** $89/month (recurring)
- **Annual option:** $890/year ($74/month — save $178, use to close hesitant leads)

## Why These Numbers
- $89/month is below the "feels expensive" threshold for small business owners
- $450 setup is a commitment filter — weeds out low-intent leads
- Combined, it's less than one missed service call ($200-$2,000 per job)
- Competitors charge $200-$500/month — we're positioned as accessible

## Negotiation Floor
- Never go below $69/month (breaks unit economics on support costs)
- Setup fee can be waived for strong referrals or 6-month prepay
- Offer annual pricing before discounting monthly rate
- If asked for a trial: offer 14-day money-back guarantee instead

## Upsell Path (future)
- Multi-location: $49/month per additional location
- SMS integration: +$29/month
- CRM integration (HubSpot/Salesforce): +$49/month
- White-label reseller license: $299/month

## Pricing Psychology
- Lead with the monthly cost ($89), not the setup fee
- Frame against what they're losing: "One missed HVAC call is $500. We're $89/month."
- Use the annual option as a close: "If you commit to the year, I'll waive the setup fee."
- Never apologize for the price — own it with confidence
